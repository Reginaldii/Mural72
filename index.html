Reginald L. Carter II is the artist and founder behind MURAL.72 — a multidisciplinary creative studio specializing in large-scale murals, custom painted installations, and branded visual environments. Based in the Lehigh Valley, PA.

From institutional commissions like the St. Luke's University Health Network Enchanted Forest to gym identity murals, botanical interiors, and fine art canvases — every project is original, hand-crafted, and built to define a space.

The studio works with gyms, restaurants, event venues, music artists, nightlife brands, healthcare systems, and sponsors to create environments people remember.

"The best spaces don't just hold events — they become the event."
